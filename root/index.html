New Insights

We are back again for our third season in FRC as team 5809. In upcoming news... in the remaining offseason we're competing at events such as K-Cup, and have secured a spot at the Heartland Regional in March.

We are very excited to announce our new partnership with Solidworks Corporation! Their software in 3D CAD and their entire Robotics Suite of software tools have completely changed our outlook on design this year.

Click here for the 2018 official Power Up game hint video!

Our 2017 season in FIRST Steamworks was full of surprises and heartbrakes. After two successful competitions in Kansas City and Iowa, we were unfortunately only 1 spot away from our ticket to Worlds, but next year we come back better prepared than ever.

In addition to our FRC robot this year, we are proud to be involved with Battlebots and underwater robotics at OpenROV.

MORE ABOUT ROCKHURST

Rockhurst High School is a Jesuit prepatory for young men based out of Kansas City, Missouri. The 1910 establishment values the essential teaching of being "men for others," and has has continued to instill this value in thousands of young men for over a century. Rockhurst students are highly dedicated to ignation values of "Ad Majorem Dei Gloriam," meaning "For the Greater Glory of God."
